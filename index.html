about us

In every corner of Germany there is a pub where the locals come to their
home. In fact, the pub is the perfect home. Without wife, children and pets.
Pub sports bar is your home, where you are not required to do anything. Find
your freedom with a pub sports bar.

The pub sports bar menu serves traditional European cuisine from the best
chefs: a variety of snacks, salad mixes of terrific combinations of flavors,
hearty steaks, large meat boards, signature meat on the ribs from our chef.

The main thing in any pub is the beer! The pub sports bar features 8 beers,
including live beer Khmelny Lion, specially brewed at a brewery in Munich.
Pub sports bar is a hearty atmosphere and home comfort !!!!!!!

We invite you to dive into the atmosphere of a traditional German pub. Here
you will be greeted by a cozy atmosphere and always friendly, hospitable,
and friendly staff. The original interior of the old pub contributes
to a pleasant pastime.
